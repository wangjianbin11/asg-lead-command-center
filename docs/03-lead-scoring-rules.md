# Lead Scoring Rules

Total score: 100.

| Dimension | Max | Meaning |
|---|---:|---|
| Sourcing Need Score | 20 | Need for China sourcing, supplier search, low MOQ, or custom products. |
| Fulfillment Pain Score | 20 | Evidence of shipping delay, unstable fulfillment, slow tracking, or high returns. |
| Custom Packaging Score | 15 | Need for custom packaging, private label, logo, inserts, labeling, or bundles. |
| Store Maturity Score | 15 | Signs of real orders, growth, SKU depth, product pages, and brand awareness. |
| Contactability Score | 15 | Available email, LinkedIn, WhatsApp, website form, or verified contact path. |
| ASG Service Fit Score | 15 | ASG can provide an obvious service match without overpromising. |

## Priority Mapping

| Priority | Score | Action |
|---|---:|---|
| A | 80-100 | Same-day salesperson review and contact. |
| B | 60-79 | Enter normal outreach or follow-up pool. |
| C | 40-59 | Convert to content opportunity or observe. |
| D | 0-39 | Do not spend salesperson time. |

## High-Value Signals

- Complete product pages and visible SKU structure.
- Shopify or WooCommerce storefront.
- Clear brand positioning.
- Evidence of shipping delay, supplier issue, QC issue, packaging need, MOQ problem, or scaling pressure.
- China sourcing or 1688-style supply chain need.
- Contact path is public and usable.

## Low-Value Signals

- No website or no product.
- Only asks for the lowest price.
- One-test-order-only with no plan.
- No usable contact path.
- Product does not fit ASG fulfillment.
- Asks only for free service.
- Not an eCommerce seller.
