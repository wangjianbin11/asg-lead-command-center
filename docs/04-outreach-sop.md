# Outreach SOP

V1 outreach is draft-only. AI prepares a message, but a salesperson must approve and send it manually.

## First Touch Rules

1. Keep the message short.
2. Personalize from public information only.
3. Do not claim the customer has a problem unless evidence clearly says so.
4. Mention one relevant ASG capability.
5. Use a low-pressure CTA.
6. Do not include exaggerated shipping, price, or service claims.
7. Write the result to `Outreach Task`.
8. Set `Approval Status = Pending Review` by default.

## Review Steps

1. Salesperson opens `Pending Review`.
2. Checks lead score, reasoning, evidence text, and contact path.
3. Edits the draft in `Human Edited Version`.
4. Sets `Approval Status = Approved`.
5. Sends manually through the selected channel.
6. Updates `Send Status`, `Send Date`, `Result`, and `Next Follow-up Date`.

## Blocked Cases

Do not contact when:
- Evidence is weak and the lead is C/D.
- Website or contact path is invalid.
- The draft depends on facts not present in the lead record.
- The channel requires platform actions that violate rules or require login bypass.
