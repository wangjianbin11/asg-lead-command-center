# Content Engine

The content engine converts real lead signals, objections, and customer replies into useful content opportunities.

## Inputs

- `Pain Signal` from Lead Pool.
- `Evidence Text` from source pages.
- `Message Content` from Conversation Log.
- `Intent`, `Urgency`, and `Next Action` from reply classification.

## Output Formats

- SEO blog.
- LinkedIn post.
- Reddit answer draft.
- Quora answer draft.
- Short video script.
- Email newsletter.
- Case study idea.
- Comparison content.

## Rules

- Every content idea must point back to a real lead or conversation.
- Do not invent a customer case.
- Do not publish AI drafts without review.
- Do not pretend to be a customer or third party.
- Content should solve a real dropshipping, sourcing, fulfillment, QC, packaging, or logistics problem.

## Daily Target

Generate at least 5 content opportunities from real signals, then manually pick 3-5 for production.
